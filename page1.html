What is Anime ?

Anime (アニメ) is Japanese for 'animation'. In Japan, anime is the word used for all animation.

Anime describes all animated works, regardless of style or origin. However, outside of Japan and in English,
anime is colloquial for Japanese animation and refers specifically to animation produced in Japan.
Anime is distributed theatrically, through television broadcasts, directly to home media, and over the Internet.
